STORY AS BELOW
There is a kindly family live in a small town.
The family members: father-John, mother-Emily, son-Jeff, daughter:Kathy

One day, they go to a shopping mall. In the fresh market, this family find something special, a glowing guacamole.

The glowing guacamole spoke to them in its creamy green voice.
It said, "I am he who travels. Speak, so that I may remember thee and spare your souls in the coming fray.

The family decided to buy the glowing guacamole. They ate it for dinner that night.
